TOP UK SCAM TYPES & RED FLAGS:
- Bank impersonation: asks to move money, send courier, urgent account freeze
- HMRC: threatens arrest, demands gift card/crypto payment
- Pig butchering: romance + crypto investment, too good to be true returns
- WhatsApp OTP: "sent you code by mistake", asks to forward SMS code
- Delivery scam: small fee to release parcel, fake Royal Mail/DPD/Evri
- Investment fraud: guaranteed returns, FCA-unregistered firm, pressure to invest
- Fake job: pay fee to unlock earnings, WhatsApp group with fake members
- Romance scam: never video calls, asks for money after weeks of chat
- SIM swap: sudden loss of signal, locked out of banking
- Phishing: link to fake gov.uk / bank site, asks for credentials
- Tech support: pop-up saying PC infected, asks for remote access
- Crypto fraud: fake exchange, wallet issues, must pay fee to withdraw
- CEO fraud: urgent wire transfer request from "boss"
- Advance fee: must pay upfront to receive prize/inheritance
- Council tax scam: fake HMRC/council demanding immediate payment

UNIVERSAL RED FLAGS:
- Urgency / threat / countdown timer
- Requests for passwords, OTP codes, sort codes, account numbers
- Payment by gift card, crypto, bank transfer to new account
- Won't video call / always has excuse
- Too good to be true (guaranteed returns, free money)
- Asks to keep secret / don't tell family
